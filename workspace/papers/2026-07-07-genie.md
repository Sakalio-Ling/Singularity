# P-010: Generative Interactive Environments (Genie)

**Title**: Generative Interactive Environments
**Year**: 2024
**Link**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2402.01604
**Authors**: Jake Bruce, Michael Dennis, et al.
**Priority**: P5

## Core Method
Video generation model trained to produce interactive, playable environments from video data.

## Key Contributions
- Learned playable environments from unlabeled video
- Latent action model discovers action space automatically
- Foundation for world model research

## Relevance to Singularity
- **Long-term reference only**: Not directly applicable to current architecture
- **World models**: Could inform future environment simulation for planning
- **M6+ vision**: Relevant if we pursue visual understanding of Minecraft worlds
