# P-014: Tree of Thoughts (ToT)

**Title**: Tree of Thoughts: Deliberate Problem Solving with Large Language Models
**Year**: 2023
**Link**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2305.10601
**Authors**: Shunyu Yao, Dian Yu, et al.
**Priority**: P3

## Core Method
Tree-structured exploration of reasoning paths: LLM generates multiple reasoning branches, evaluates each, and backtracks when needed.

## Key Contributions
- Enables deliberate problem solving vs left-to-right generation
- BFS/DFS search over reasoning paths
- Significantly improves tasks requiring exploration or planning ahead

## Relevance to Singularity
- **Planner module**: ToT can improve long-horizon planning (e.g., diamond obtainment requires 10+ steps)
- **Re-planning**: When first plan fails, ToT-style branching explores alternatives
- **Implementation**: Could integrate as a planning mode for complex goals (M4+)
